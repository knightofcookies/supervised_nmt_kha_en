Ha u bnai May 1889 la thung ia u Sahep John Eliot kum u Director General ba nyngkong jong ki Observatories ha ka nongbah Calcutta kaba mynshuwa .
Une u samla pule uba dang shah sumar mynta ha Dhaka Medical College Hospital u la shah buh ha ka life support ha ka sngi Nyngkong .
Ka la don ka jingbitar kaba jur hadien kata .
Kumta , kiba bun ki skulbah ba pawnam ki ai jingmut kyrpang ban pynroi ia ka sap jong ki samla pule .
